Eye Care Tips

  • Take regular breaks using the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  • Maintain proper lighting to reduce eye strain.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your eyes moist.
  • Wear sunglasses to protect your eyes from UV rays.
  • Get regular eye check-ups from an eye care professional.
